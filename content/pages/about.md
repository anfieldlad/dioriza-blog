I'm Bobby Ananta Dioriza.

In professional life, I work as a **Technical Lead / Software Engineer**. If you want the more professional version of me, you can also visit my [resume](https://bobby.dioriza.com). But this blog is really meant to show the other side of me, the side that is easier to explain through what I watch, where I go, and what I keep returning to.

I like **movies**, **series**, **travel**, and **football**. That is the real backbone of this site.

The yearly reviews are my way of remembering what stayed with me from a particular year, whether it was a blockbuster, a season finale, or a show that completely took over my mind for a while. The travel posts are a different kind of archive: places I visited, routes I took, and small details I might want to remember before the next trip.

So while my day job lives in technology, this blog is more personal than professional. It is a place to keep stories, preferences, impressions, and a record of the things I genuinely enjoy.
