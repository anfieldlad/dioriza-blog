A short Singapore getaway that was already booked, but ultimately cancelled because of COVID-19.

## Bookings

- **Route:** Jakarta to Singapore
- **Flight:** Jetstar return flight (`IDR 589,649`) via Traveloka PayLater promo
- **Stay:** BOND Boutique Capsule Hotel for 4 nights (`IDR 398,412`) via Traveloka PayLater promo

## Planned Attractions

- **Universal Studios Singapore** (`IDR 399,130`) via Traveloka PayLater promo
- **ArtScience Museum: Disney: Magic of Animation** (`IDR 129,000`) via Traveloka promo

## Total Paid Before Cancellation

- **Flight:** `IDR 589,649`
- **Accommodation:** `IDR 398,412`
- **Attractions:** `IDR 528,130`
- **Total:** `IDR 1,516,191`

---

## Notes

The highlight of this plan was how unusually low the total budget was, especially because the flight, hotel, and attractions had already been paid at promo prices. It felt like I had managed to put together a very affordable Singapore trip. Unfortunately, it never happened because COVID-19 changed everything just before the trip could take place.
